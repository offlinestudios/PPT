Our Story

Passport Photo Toronto (PPT) is a top-rated passport and visa photo service located inside Offline Studios, a creative photography space in the heart of downtown Toronto. Owned and operated by professional photographer Julian Ross, PPT has quickly become one of the most trusted destinations for passport photos in Toronto's Entertainment District.

With over 7 years of full-time photography experience, Julian launched Passport Photo Toronto after noticing consistent demand from walk-in visitors seeking passport and visa photo services. What began as an unplanned side offering at Offline Studios soon evolved into a specialized service trusted by thousands.

PPT started with a focus on Canadian passport photos, but quickly expanded to offer compliant visa photos for over 40 international countries. Julian personally researched each country's requirements to ensure all photos meet strict government standards.
