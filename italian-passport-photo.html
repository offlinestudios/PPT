Common passport photo Mistakes to Avoid

Many citizenship applications are delayed or rejected due to photo issues. Common mistakes include:

  • Incorrect photo size
  • Shadows on face or background
  • Wearing glasses (no longer permitted in passport photos)
  • Improper facial expression (smiling, frowning, etc.)
  • Poor lighting or contrast
  • Photos taken too long ago (must be within 6 months)
  • Missing photographer information on the back of the photo

Our professional service helps you avoid these common pitfalls, ensuring your application proceeds smoothly.
