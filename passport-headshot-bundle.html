What to Wear

For the best results in your professional headshot:

  • Wear solid colors that complement your skin tone (avoid busy patterns)
  • Choose professional attire appropriate for your industry
  • Avoid white or very light colors that may blend with the background
  • Business casual or business formal works best
  • Keep jewelry and accessories minimal and professional
