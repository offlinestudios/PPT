Photo Requirements at a Glance

Standard size: 50 × 70 mm (Canadian) or 2" × 2" (US/international)
Background: Plain white or off-white, no shadows
Face: Centred, neutral expression, eyes open and clearly visible
Lighting: Even, no glare, no flash reflections
Recency: Taken within the last 6 months
Print quality: High-resolution photographic paper — no home printing
