Our Photo Process

1

Consultation

We begin by understanding your specific photo requirements based on the document type and country. Our experts are familiar with the latest regulations for all major passport and visa types.

2

Professional Photography

Our photographers use professional equipment and proper lighting to capture your photo according to the specific requirements. We ensure correct posture, expression, and background.

3

Verification & Editing

We carefully verify that your photo meets all technical specifications including size, background, head position, and facial expression. Minor adjustments are made if necessary.

4

Printing & Digital Delivery

Your photos are printed on high-quality photo paper at the exact dimensions required. We also provide a digital copy that can be used for online applications.

Our Compliance Guarantee

At Passport Photo Toronto, we stand behind the quality and compliance of our photos. We guarantee that all photos taken at our studio will meet the official requirements for your specific document type.

If your photo is rejected by any official agency due to non-compliance with their photo requirements, we will retake your photos at no additional cost. This guarantee applies to all passport, visa, ID, and license photos taken at our studio.

Our photographers stay up-to-date with the latest requirements from embassies, consulates, and government agencies to ensure your photos are always compliant with current regulations.
