  • Eyes open and clearly visible
  • No shadows on the face or background

Chinese Visa Application Information

While we specialize in visa photos, here's some general information about Chinese visa applications from Canada:

  • Chinese visa applications from Canada must be submitted in person or through an authorized visa agency
  • The Chinese Visa Application Service Center (CVASC) in Toronto serves Ontario, Manitoba, and Nunavut
  • Processing times vary by visa type, but typically take 4-7 working days for standard applications
  • Express service (2-3 working days) and rush service (1 working day) are available for additional fees
  • Two identical photos are typically required for the application

For the most current information about Chinese visa applications, please visit the official Chinese Visa Application Service Center website .
