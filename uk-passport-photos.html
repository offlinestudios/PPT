UK Passport Photo Specifications

UK passport photos have specific requirements that must be followed precisely:

  • Size: 45mm high x 35mm wide
  • Head Size: The head must be between 29mm and 34mm from chin to crown
  • Background: Plain light grey or cream background
  • Pose: Looking straight at the camera with a neutral expression and mouth closed
  • Eyes: Open and clearly visible, no hair covering the eyes
  • Glasses: No glasses unless medically necessary (with a doctor's note)
  • Head Coverings: Not allowed except for religious reasons
  • Shadows: No shadows on the face or background
  • Quality: Clear, in focus, and in color
  • Recency: Taken within the last month
