Key Photo Requirements

  • Size: 2x2 inches (51x51mm)
  • Background: White or light-colored
  • Professional appearance

University Application Photo Requirements

Different universities and academic programs have specific requirements for application photos. Our service ensures your photos will comply with common requirements, including:

  • Standard passport size (2x2 inches or 51x51mm, or as specified by your institution)
  • Recent photograph taken within the last 6 months
  • Color photo with a white or light-colored background
  • Full face view directly facing the camera
  • Neutral facial expression or natural smile
  • Eyes open and clearly visible
  • No shadows on the face or background
  • Professional appearance suitable for academic applications
  • Proper lighting with no red-eye effect

University Application Photo Tips

To ensure the best results for your university application photos, consider these tips:

  • Dress professionally (business casual or formal attire is recommended)
  • Choose solid-colored clothing (avoid patterns, logos, or bright colors)
  • Ensure hair is neat and does not cover the face or eyes
  • Minimal makeup is recommended for a natural, professional appearance
  • Remove glasses if possible, unless required for medical reasons
  • Check with your specific institution for any unique photo requirements
