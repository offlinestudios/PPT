United States Travel Visa Photo Requirements

U.S. Department of State has specific requirements for travel visa photos. Our service ensures your photos will comply with all these requirements:

  • Size: 2" x 2" (51mm x 51mm)
  • Head size: Between 1 inch and 1 3/8 inches (22mm-35mm) or 50%-69% of image height
  • Recent photograph taken within the last 6 months
  • Color photo with a plain white or off-white background
  • Full face view directly facing the camera
  • Neutral facial expression with both eyes open
  • No shadows on the face or background
  • No eyeglasses (except for rare medical circumstances)
  • No head coverings (except for religious reasons)
  • Proper lighting with no red-eye effect

    Common travel visa photo Mistakes to Avoid

    Many citizenship applications are delayed or rejected due to photo issues. Common mistakes include:

    • Incorrect photo size
    • Shadows on face or background
    • Wearing glasses (no longer permitted in travel visa photos)
    • Improper facial expression (smiling, frowning, etc.)
    • Poor lighting or contrast
    • Photos taken too long ago (must be within 6 months)
    • Missing photographer information on the back of the photo

    Our professional service helps you avoid these common pitfalls, ensuring your application proceeds smoothly.
